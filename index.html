The numeric chips show the real Bloch vector ⟨X⟩, ⟨Y⟩, ⟨Z⟩ and length r. The sphere is a 2D perspective projection, so Y-depth can look visually shorter even when r=1.

What to notice

SuperpositionH spreads amplitude across basis states. Probabilities are squared magnitudes of amplitudes.
PhaseZ, S, and T can change phase without changing probabilities until later gates interfere.
EntanglementBell/GHZ states make individual Bloch vectors mixed: r drops below 1.
